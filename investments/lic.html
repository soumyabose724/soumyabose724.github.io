Honest read: Traditional LIC endowment/money-back plans bundle insurance and investment together, and typically deliver 5–6% IRR over the full term — below PPF, FD, and most debt mutual funds, even before tax. The insurance cover (₹10L) is also thin for the premium paid; a comparable term insurance plan would cost a fraction of ₹45,239/year for the same cover. Most fee-only advisors recommend buying pure term insurance separately and investing the premium difference in PPF/FD/index funds — that combination usually outperforms this policy on both cover and returns. Since this policy is already 10 years in, factor in surrender-value loss before deciding whether to continue, surrender, or convert to paid-up.
