Why this exists: These bonds defer Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) tax on a property sale under Section 54EC — the gain must be invested within 6 months of transfer, in bonds locked in for 5 years. The 5.25% p.a. interest is fully taxable, so the real benefit is the tax deferral/exemption on the capital gain itself, not the coupon. Redeeming or transferring before the 5-year lock-in reverses the LTCG exemption in the year of redemption.
