Tax Treatment
1. At Exercise — Perquisite Tax
Taxed as salary income (perquisite) in the year you exercise: (FMV at exercise − Strike Price) × Shares Exercised.
Tranche 1 example: ($22.00 − $15.00) × 100 = $700.00 at ₹83.10/USD = ₹58,170 added to Vikas's salary income for AY 2025-26 and taxed at his slab rate.
2. At Sale — Capital Gains
When the exercised shares are eventually sold: (Sale Price − FMV at Exercise) × Shares. Since NMBS is US-listed, this is a foreign capital asset — LTCG at 12.5% (no indexation) if held >24 months from the exercise date, else STCG at slab rate.
