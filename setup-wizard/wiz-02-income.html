NEXT_MONTH: Income received in June is counted in July's budget. Use this for salary that arrives at month-end but funds the following month's expenses.
The routing rule determines which month's budget the income flows into. Most salaries use NEXT_MONTH — you're paid in April for May's expenses. Rental and interest income typically use SAME_MONTH.
