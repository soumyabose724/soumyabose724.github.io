Indexation removal — transition rule: Budget 2024 (23 Jul 2024) removed indexation benefit for most non-equity LTCG (gold, debt, unlisted assets) going forward, replacing it with a flat 12.5% rate. A specific transition rule lets individuals/HUFs choose the higher of (a) 12.5% without indexation, or (b) 20% with indexation, for land/buildings acquired before 23 Jul 2024. That transition choice does not apply to gold, REITs, bonds, or SGBs — those follow the new flat-rate regime directly, except SGB's own maturity exemption, which is a separate provision.
