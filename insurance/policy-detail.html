Cancel this policy?

If you cancel or let HDFC Life Click 2 Protect Super lapse:

  • Your family loses the entire ₹1,00,00,000 death benefit immediately.
  • The ₹10,00,000 Critical Illness rider and ₹25,00,000 Accidental Death rider are cancelled too.
  • Any future premium already paid for this cycle is forfeited — pure term plans have no surrender value.
  • Re-buying cover later means new medical underwriting and a higher premium, since Vikas will be older.
  • Your current HLV-based coverage gap (see HLV Calculator) will widen by the full sum assured.

This action cannot be undone once the insurer processes it.
