VDA Tax Rules — Unusually Punitive
•Flat 30% tax on every crypto gain, regardless of your income slab or holding period — there's no LTCG/STCG distinction like equity.
•No loss set-off. Vikas's ₹6,500 ETH loss cannot reduce his taxable BTC gain of ₹90,000 — each VDA transaction is taxed independently, and losses can't offset gains even within crypto, let alone against other income.
•1% TDS is deducted at source on transfers above ₹50,000/year (specified persons) or ₹10,000/year (everyone else) — adjustable against final tax liability via Form 26AS, but it hits cash flow immediately.
•Estimated tax on this book: 30% of ₹90,000 = ₹27,000 (the ETH loss gives no relief).
